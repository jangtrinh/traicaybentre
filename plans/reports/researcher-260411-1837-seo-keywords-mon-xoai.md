## UNRESOLVED QUESTIONS & CAVEATS

### Data Limitations

1. **Search Volume Accuracy:** Web search results do NOT provide exact Google Keyword Planner data. Estimates are based on:
   - Blog/article count (more articles = higher demand signal)
   - Content freshness (2024–2025 publications indicate ongoing search interest)
   - User engagement signals (social media mentions, TikTok trending)
   - **Actual volumes require Google Ads KP or SEMrush/Ahrefs subscription**

2. **Regional Variance:** Search volumes assume Vietnam-wide queries. Mango recipes show regional preference:
   - Mekong Delta (Bến Tre focus) — higher local search for "xoài bến tre" + recipes
   - Northern Vietnam — preference for "xoài đâm" & "mắm ruốc" dips (not "xoài lắc")
   - Southern Vietnam — higher "xoài lắc" + "gỏi xoài" demand
   - **Consider geo-targeting in future analytics**

3. **Competitive Landscape:** Current top 10 results for high-volume keywords (e.g., "nước ép xoài") are mostly:
   - Recipe blogs (Dienmayxanh, Eva, Mytour, Huongnghiepaau, FPT Shop) — general food sites
   - ZERO specialized mango/ben-tre sourced content
   - **High opportunity to rank in niche "bến tre mango" + "recipe" combo**

4. **Seasonality:** Mango demand peaks:
   - April–June (harvest season, fresh supply)
   - Hot months (May–September) — beverage demand spikes
   - Tet (January/February) — mứt/jam gifting demand (already covered)
   - **Consider seasonal content calendar adjustments**

### Content Publishing Risks

1. **Roundup Cannibalization:** 30-recipe roundup already covers all 5 proposed new articles at 1–2 paragraph depth. Publishing dedicated articles may dilute traffic from roundup. Mitigation: Link roundup → detailed articles (funnel upgrade approach).

2. **Low-Priority Keywords:** "canh chua xoài" (sour soup) estimates ~50–100/mo searches — **too low for standalone article**. Keep in roundup, monitor for seasonal spikes.

3. **Competitive Pricing:** Ice cream & mousse articles compete with commercial brands (Baskin-Robbins, local cafés). Content angle should be "make at home easily" to avoid lost sales intent.
