### Cold Chain Protocol (Farm to Table)

| Stage | Temp | Duration | Ripeness | Container | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Pick (farm)** | Ambient 28–32°C | Same day | Mature green (55–70% yellow) | Rattan basket | Don't pick overripe; will rot in transit |
| **Cooling (vựa)** | 18–22°C room | 2–4 hrs | Immature green | Ventilated carton | Slow cooling reduces shock |
| **Storage (vựa)** | 12–15°C | 1–7 days | Green | Carton, lining paper | Best: 12–14°C; hold before order confirmation |
| **Transit to HCM** | 8–12°C (insulated) | 24 hrs | Green | Foam-lined carton | Vibration padding critical; rough roads = bruise |
| **Transit to Hanoi** | 8–12°C (reefer truck) | 48 hrs | Green | Foam-lined carton | Longer; maintain 8–10°C to slow respiration |
| **Retail display** | 18–22°C ambient | 1–3 days | Mature green | Basket / shelf | Transition to warmer environment begins ripening |
| **Consumer shelf** | 20–24°C room | 5–7 days | Green→Ripe | Paper bag or counter | Natural ripening; ethylene buildup speeds process |
| **Fridge (extended)** | 4–6°C | 7–14 days | Ripe | Crisper drawer | Cold halts ripening; pulls moisture; slight pitting risk |

### Ripeness Indicators (Practical Guide)

**Green Stage (2–3 days old from farm):**
- Color: Uniform green or 10–20% yellow blush
- Touch: Firm, no give
- Smell: Neutral, slight fruity
- Use: Cooking, gỏi, chế biến

**Mature Green (4–5 days old):**
- Color: 30–50% yellow
- Touch: Slight softness on cusp; still firm overall
- Smell: Faint sweet
- Use: Will ripen in 1–2 days; good for shipping to rural areas

**Ripe (6–8 days old):**
- Color: 70–100% yellow/gold
- Touch: Soft on stem end; slight give along sides
- Smell: Strong fruity, sweet aroma
- Use: Eat immediately; maximum flavor intensity
- **Taste check:** Slice; if Na is detectable after sweetness, it's genuine Bến Tre

**Overripe (9+ days old):**
- Color: Yellow with brown spots / dark patches
- Touch: Very soft, spongy; flesh interior darkening
- Smell: Fermented / alcoholic
- Use: Discard or compost; risk of mold/yeast contamination

### Storage Mistakes & Solutions

| Problem | Cause | Fix |
|---------|-------|-----|
| Fruit ripens too fast (2–3 days) | Stored above 24°C | Keep room temp 18–22°C; avoid direct sun |
| Fruit won't ripen (10+ days) | Stored below 12°C too long | Move to 22–24°C room; ethylene gas (banana nearby) speeds it |
| Brown spots inside / external pitting | Cold damage during transit | Insulate carton better; avoid cold <8°C |
| Soft spots / mold | High humidity + bruising | Keep dry; ventilate; don't stack >6 layers |
| Bitter aftertaste when ripe | Over-picked (immature) | Buy from reputable seller; mature green only |
