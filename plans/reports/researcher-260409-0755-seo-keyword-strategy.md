**1. Comparison Keywords** — Not targeting
- "xoài tứ quý vs xoài cát hòa lộc" (differentiate vs competitor variety)
- "xoài bến tre vs xoài đài loan" (combat imported mangoes)
- "xoài tứ quý vs xoài cát chu" (local competitor comparison)

**2. Question Keywords** — Partially covered in FAQ, not as H1/H2
- "Xoài Tứ Quý ăn chín hay xanh?" (exists in FAQ, weak ranking)
- "Xoài sỉ tối thiểu mua bao nhiêu?" (exists in FAQ)

## Competitive Intelligence

### Direct Competitors (Domains Owning Keywords)
- **xoaituquythanhphu.com** (linked in JSON-LD sameAs)
  - Likely ranking for "xoài tứ quý thạnh phú" + brand name
  - **Action:** Outrank with broader keyword coverage + content depth
- **Regional government/tourism sites** (Bến Tre gov domains)
  - Own "Bến Tre trái cây", regional agriculture content
  - **Action:** Differentiate with retail/e-commerce angle (they are B2B/informational)
- **E-commerce platforms** (Shopee, Tiki, Lazada)
  - Rank for generic "xoài", "trái cây", "xoài sỉ"
  - **Action:** Target specific brand keyword ("tứ quý") + long-tail geo combinations

### Competitive Gaps (Opportunities)
1. **No site ranks for "xoài tứ quý vs [competitor]"** — Zero comparison content in Vietnamese mango market
2. **Shipping/logistics angle untapped** — "ship mang bị dập không" has intent but no good answers
3. **Customization/B2B angle weak** — "quy cách riêng", "đóng thùng tuỳ chọn" are unique selling points not marketed via SEO
