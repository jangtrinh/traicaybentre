## J. MANGO STAGE REFERENCE GUIDE

### **Ripe Mango** (Yellow/Golden, Soft)
Best for: Desserts, drinks, smoothies, salads (when mixed), ceviche, sauces
- Sweet, creamy texture
- Natural sweetness
- Easier to blend/puree

### **Green/Unripe Mango** (Hard, Sour)
Best for: Pickles, chutneys, salads (when raw), curries, appetizers
- Tangy/sour flavor
- Firm texture resists cooking
- Better for preservation
- Common in Southeast Asian & Indian cuisine

### **Frozen Mango**
Best for: Sorbet, ice cream, smoothies, popsicles
- Extends season
- Perfect texture for frozen desserts
- No need for added ice

### **Dried Mango**
Best for: Leather, candy, fruit roll-ups, snacks, some preserves
- Concentrated sweetness
- Long shelf-life (2-3 years)
- Chewy or hard options
