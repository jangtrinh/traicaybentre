## Content Gap Analysis

### Underserved Queries (High Intent, Low Content Quality)

1. **Recipe Guides with Problem-Solving**
   - "rau câu dừa không chảy nước" (non-melting jelly) — competitors publish basic recipes; opportunity for **technical deep-dive** with video/photos
   - "rau câu dừa vón cục" (clumping issues) — FAQ-style content solving common mistakes
   - **Gap:** Detailed trouble-shooting + chemistry explanation missing

2. **Regional Comparison Content**
   - "dừa bến tre vs trà vinh" — exists but mostly government/trade sites; opportunity for **consumer-focused comparison** (taste, price, sourcing)
   - **Gap:** Retail buyer's perspective missing

3. **B2B + Transactional**
   - "dừa sỉ bến tre" — mostly marketplace listings; opportunity for **sourcing guide** (min order, pricing, logistics, quality standards)
   - "nhà cung cấp dừa tươi" — directory-style opportunity
   - **Gap:** Trust signals (certifications, MOQs, lead times) sparse

4. **Seasonal/Trend Content**
   - "rau câu dừa mùa hè" — no specific seasonal content; opportunity for **summer drink roundup**
   - **Gap:** Trending seasonal angle unused
